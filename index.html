About

My name is Sean Gosha. I am an aspiring software developer from Appleton, Wisconsin. Understanding computers and technology has always come naturally to me. My journey into the world of software development began in 2020 when I started learning C# by developing games in Unity.

Currently, I'm working towards an Associates in Software Development at Fox Valley Technical College. While I haven't had the chance to work in the industry just yet, I'm a new programmer eager to gain professional experience. I'm open to learning anything new and tend to invest a lot of time and effort in expanding my knowledge.

Outside of coding, you'll often find me skateboarding, enjoying a game of disc golf, or simply spending quality time with family and friends.
I appreciate you taking the time to get to know me. If you have any questions or just want to chat, don't hesitate to get in touch!
